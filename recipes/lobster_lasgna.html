Lobster Lasagna Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  1. 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  2. 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  3. 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  4. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. 2 large eggs
  6. 1 medium onion, minced
  7. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  8. 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  9. 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  10. 2 (16 ounce) jars Alfredo pasta sauce
  11. 16 no-boil lasagna noodles
  12. 2 pounds cooked and cubed lobster meat
  13. 1 (10 ounce) package baby spinach leaves
